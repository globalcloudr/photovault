# Canopy Brand Strategy

Date: 2026-03-11

## Purpose

Define a clear brand architecture for the current PhotoVault product and future school-focused products so naming, domain decisions, and product positioning stay consistent as the portfolio grows.

## Core Decision

- `Canopy` should be treated as the umbrella brand.
- `PhotoVault` should remain the current product name.
- `usecanopy.school` is a strong candidate for the umbrella brand domain.

This avoids forcing PhotoVault to carry the weight of future products that are not directly related to digital asset management.

## Recommended Brand Architecture

- `Canopy` = umbrella brand / platform brand
- `PhotoVault` = dedicated marketing and brand asset product

Recommended public presentation:

- `PhotoVault by Canopy`

This keeps current momentum on PhotoVault while giving room for a broader product family.

## Why This Structure Is Better

### 1. It scales beyond one product

The planned roadmap already points beyond a single DAM product. Future ideas include:

- website CMS for schools
- internal knowledge assistants
- structured data query assistants
- newsletter platform
- client portals
- broader marketing platform
- WordPress-related tooling

Those products do not fit naturally under the `PhotoVault` name.

### 2. It keeps PhotoVault sharply positioned

PhotoVault is strongest when positioned as:

- a dedicated marketing and brand asset system for schools

not:

- a general-purpose file storage replacement
- a full collaboration suite
- a Google Drive or Box replacement

Keeping PhotoVault as a focused product preserves clarity.

### 3. It reduces future rebranding risk

If multiple tools are launched later, a platform-level brand already exists. That means future naming can stay coherent instead of requiring a major renaming effort later.

## Domain Strategy

### Recommended umbrella domain

- `usecanopy.school`

Why it works:

- clear school-sector signal because of `.school`
- modern product/domain feel
- broad enough to support multiple products
- strong enough to become the primary company/platform identity

### Recommended product naming on the umbrella domain

Examples:

- `PhotoVault by Canopy`
- `Canopy CMS`
- `Canopy Assist`
- `Canopy Portals`
- `Canopy Campaigns`

The naming system should remain simple:

- umbrella brand = `Canopy`
- product descriptor = specific product name

## Positioning Implication for PhotoVault

PhotoVault should continue to be positioned as:

- the simple, powerful way to manage school marketing and brand assets

This means:

- schools may still use Google Drive or other tools for general documents
- PhotoVault becomes the dedicated layer for:
  - approved photos
  - logos
  - templates
  - campaign assets
  - share-ready media
  - brand-consistent content

This positioning is stronger and more defensible than trying to compete as a broad file-storage system.

## Recommended Messaging Model

### Umbrella brand message

Canopy should be presented as:

- a platform for practical school tools
- purpose-built for adult schools and school teams
- focused on communications, marketing, operations, and digital workflows

### PhotoVault message

PhotoVault should be presented as:

- the dedicated marketing and brand asset system within the Canopy ecosystem

## Product Family Direction

Suggested future portfolio model:

- `PhotoVault by Canopy`
- `Canopy CMS`
- `Canopy Assist`
- `Canopy Data`
- `Canopy Portals`
- `Canopy Campaigns`

These names are only directional, but the structure is sound:

- umbrella brand first
- clear product role second

## Near-Term Recommendation

Do not rename PhotoVault right now.

Recommended immediate path:

- keep building and launching `PhotoVault`
- use `Canopy` as the strategic parent brand
- reserve the umbrella brand for future platform expansion

That preserves launch momentum and avoids unnecessary product churn.

## Practical Rollout Plan

### Now

- keep the app/product name as `PhotoVault`
- continue homepage and buyer-facing copy using the current PhotoVault positioning
- evaluate whether to reserve or purchase `usecanopy.school`

### Next

- begin using `PhotoVault by Canopy` in strategy and future-facing materials
- keep internal docs aligned to the umbrella-brand model

### Later

- launch the main platform site under `Canopy`
- present PhotoVault as the first product in the suite
- add future products under the same naming system

## Guardrails

- Do not let `Canopy` become just a domain with no brand meaning.
- Do not rename PhotoVault before launch unless there is a clear business reason.
- Do not use inconsistent parallel brands that confuse buyers.
- Do not position PhotoVault as a general document storage replacement.

## Working Conclusion

The best current strategy is:

- launch `PhotoVault`
- build `Canopy`
- let `Canopy` become the scalable umbrella for future school software products

That gives the current product a sharp story and gives the future business a clean brand system.
