# PhotoVault User Guide (School Users) - v1 Draft

## About This Guide
This is a starter guide for school staff using PhotoVault. It will be updated as new features are added.

## What Is PhotoVault?
PhotoVault helps your school team:
- create photo albums for events
- upload and organize photos
- choose album cover images
- search and review photos quickly

## Sign In
1. Open your PhotoVault login page.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. If you were invited by your school admin, use the invite email first to complete account setup.

Invited-user setup note:
- after accepting an invite, PhotoVault shows a setup progress screen while your workspace loads
- your admin may include a personalized welcome message in that setup experience

## Albums Page
The Albums page is your main dashboard.

You can:
- view all albums for your school
- open an album
- create a new album
- choose an album cover image
- use top toolbar tools:
  - search albums
  - sort albums
  - switch between grid/list views
- use left sidebar rights filter

## Create a New Album
1. Click `New album`.
2. Fill in:
- Event name
- Event date
- Rights status
- Department (if your school uses departments)
3. Click `Create album`.

## Open an Album
1. Click `Open` on any album card.
2. On the album page you can:
- upload photos
- search photos by filename/sequence
- update metadata on selected photos in bulk (tags/event type/campus/photographer)
- delete one photo or multiple selected photos
- create secure share links with optional password, expiry, and download controls
- view photos in the lightbox
- use image overlay actions (`Select`, `View`, `Download`, `More`)
- use `More` for additional actions like `Open` and `Delete`
- review album details and totals in the hero header

Share links note:
- revoked/expired links are retained for audit history for up to 1 year

## Upload Photos
You can upload in two ways:
- Click `Upload photos` and select files
- Drag and drop image files directly onto the album page

Notes:
- upload supports image files
- phone upload is supported (photo library/files, depending on your device/browser)
- folder upload is not supported yet (select files instead)
- there is no fixed in-app max file count per upload right now; for best reliability, use smaller batches on slower devices/networks
- files are added in sequence order
- upload queue shows progress and duplicate detection feedback

## Choose Album Cover
From the Albums page:
1. Click `Choose cover` on an album card.
2. Select one of that album’s photos.
3. The selected image becomes the cover preview.

## Appearance (If You Have Access)
Some users (school owners/admins) can open `Appearance` to update:
- colors
- logo (upload file directly, or provide URL)
- font fields (if used by your org)

Appearance applies to your school’s active PhotoVault organization.

## Brand Portal Collection
The `Brand Portal` collection is your shared reference area for school brand assets.

Use it to store:
- logo versions
- PDF/Doc brand guidance
- approved brand reference files for staff

## Page Layout (What You See)
- Top utility header:
  - Support link
  - user profile menu (top-right initials/avatar)
  - profile menu includes Help, Questions/feedback, Settings (Profile), and Logout
  - Billing appears in menu as disabled/coming soon
- Left sidebar: navigation and page-specific tools.
- Right content area: page title, hero section, and cards/data.

## Profile Menu (Top-Right)
Open the avatar/initials button to access personal and account actions:
- Help
- Questions / feedback
- Settings (Profile)
- Logout

If your account is super admin, the menu also includes a `Super Admin` shortcut.

## Profile Settings (Name)
To update your displayed name:
1. Open the profile menu (top-right avatar/initials).
2. Click `Settings (Profile)`.
3. Update `Display name`.
4. Click `Save profile`.

Note:
- Name changes are account-level (user profile), not organization appearance settings.
- `Appearance` is for school branding (colors, logos, fonts).

## Switching Organizations
If you have access to multiple organizations, use the active org controls in the app (Super Admin flow) before creating albums or editing appearance settings.

## Troubleshooting
- If you do not see albums:
  - confirm you are in the correct organization
  - confirm your account was added to that school
- If upload fails:
  - check file type (image)
  - retry with smaller batches
- If appearance changes do not appear:
  - save appearance settings, then refresh the page

## Need Help?
Contact your school PhotoVault administrator first.  
If the issue continues, send:
- your email address
- school name
- page URL
- a screenshot of the issue
- time the issue occurred
